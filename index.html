SHOWREEL ABOUT ME

Hello! I am a Cinematographer based in Mumbai, India. Graduating in Cinematography from the very First Batch at Whistling Woods International, Mumbai, I combine technical expertise with creativity for a vision that would resonate with the audience.

I have worked on multiple formats like Films, TV Commercials, Music videos and Shorts, to build a diverse portfolio for all my potential associations.

Having trained under Legendary Cinematographers Rajen Kothari, Binod Pradhan and various other unique directors, my journey as a cinematographer has inspired me to create my own style in capturing bleak beauty.

I strive to create ethereal and cinematic images in my pursuit for perfect visuals along with staying updated with newer tools and techniques that are constantly evolving in the Film Industry. I have a passion for capturing emotional experiences in storytelling with narrative driven imagery.

I strongly believe in serving the worldview of a story through my lens. Scroll down to have a look.
